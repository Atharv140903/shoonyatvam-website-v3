  2. Information Collected

    3. Voluntary Information: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you register on the Site, express interest in our products and Services, participate in activities on the Site, or contact us. This may include your name, password, contact details, location, date of birth, geographical information, Apple ID, gender, PAN card/Aadhaar card/election card/driving licence, GST number, identity cards or registration details issued by relevant authorities, drug licence, pharmacist registration details, photos of your pharmacy store, and any other details relevant to the Services. This information is referred to as “Personal Information”.

    We may also collect Sensitive Personal Data or Information, including but not limited to: (i) passwords; (ii) financial information (bank account or credit/debit card/virtual wallets/UPI information); (iii) inventory data, sales and purchase data, accounting information (receivables and payables); (iv) health conditions; (v) any information relating to Personal Information provided for procuring the Services; (vi) biometric information. Such information, when combined with other information available to us, can directly or indirectly identify you.

    All Personal Information provided must be true, complete, and accurate. Any changes must be promptly communicated to us.

    Browser and Device Information: We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate the Site. This may include IP address, browser and device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, device name, country, location, usage information, and technical information necessary for maintaining security and operation of the Site and for internal analytics and reporting purposes.

    Communications and Interactions: We may collect communications and interactions with you, including chats, emails, and feedback/testimonials provided to support tickets or at our request.

    Inventory Data: We may collect, store, and process inventory data of retail pharmacies to respond to queries regarding the availability of medicines/pharmaceutical products.

    • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security breaches.
