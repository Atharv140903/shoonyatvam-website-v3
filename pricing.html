Frequently asked questions

What is dawa.ai all about?

Dawa.ai is an advanced, cloud-based pharmacy management software designed to optimize and enhance the efficiency of pharmacy businesses of all sizes. It offers a comprehensive suite of features tailored to streamline operations, from inventory management to customer relationship management.

How does dawa.ai benefit your business?

Dawa.ai boosts your business by providing intelligent procurement, effective inventory management, and powerful CRM tools. It helps ensure zero loss on expired items, improves staff performance tracking, and offers GST-compliant reports, all accessible remotely via PC or mobile.

How can you subscribe to dawa.ai?

To subscribe to dawa.ai, visit the website, choose a suitable pricing plan, sign up by providing your details, and complete the payment process. Enjoy a one-month free trial to experience the full range of features before committing to a subscription.

How quickly can dawa.ai be integrated into daily business operations?

Dawa.ai can be integrated into your daily business operations almost immediately after subscription. The user-friendly interface ensures a smooth transition with minimal downtime, allowing you to start benefiting from its features right away.

What support mechanisms does dawa.ai offer in case of any queries?

Dawa.ai offers 24/7 customer support, free demo and training sessions, and a comprehensive knowledge base with instructional videos. Continuous updates and new features based on user feedback further enhance the support experience.
